FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who is this bootcamp for?

+

This 30-Day Bootcamp is specifically designed for students in Grades 5 to 10 who are struggling with mobile addiction, lack of focus, poor time management, or need constant reminders to study.

What is the schedule of the live sessions?

+

There are 4 weekly live group sessions conducted on weekends. These are interactive, fun, and designed to teach students smart study methods and self-discipline strategies.

How do daily missions work?

+

Students receive bite-sized daily challenges via our platform. These tasks take less than 15 minutes but help build consistent habits and self-responsibility.

What support will parents receive?

+

Parents receive weekly parent reports, mentor accountability follow-ups, and parenting guidelines to help support their child's habits at home without constant nagging.
