FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who is this bootcamp for?

+

This 30-Day Bootcamp is specifically designed for students in Grades 5 to 10 who struggle with screen time, lack a daily study routine, face exam anxiety, or need constant reminders from parents to open their books.

How is this different from regular tuition classes?

+

Regular tuition focuses purely on teaching school subjects (Math, Science, etc.). Mash Magic focuses on how to study. We teach the psychological foundation of focus, self-discipline, time management, and scientific study habits.

What is the schedule of the live sessions?

+

The bootcamp includes 4 live interactive weekend webinars. These are engaging sessions where we teach study science, answer student questions, and launch the upcoming week's daily mission tracker.

What if my child misses a live session?

+

No worries! All live coaching webinars are recorded and uploaded to the student dashboard within 2 hours. Your child can watch the replay anytime and complete their weekend mission.

How does the daily mentor support work?

+

Your child is assigned a dedicated Student Mentor. Every day, the mentor checks if they completed their study mission, sends encouraging messages, tracks consistency, and helps them clear learning roadblocks.

Will this program help improve my child's grades?

+

Yes. By replacing passive reading with active recall techniques and building a daily consistent 2-hour study routine, students learn more in less time, leading directly to higher academic performance.

What is included in the Weekly Parent Reports?

+

Parents receive a detailed weekly behavioral audit via WhatsApp/email. This report highlights total study hours, screen time limit consistency, streak metrics, and direct mentor suggestions for the home environment.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If your child completes the daily missions for 14 days and you don't see any positive improvement in their study consistency, simply email us for a full, prompt refund.

How much time does my child need to spend daily?

+

The daily bootcamp missions take only 15 to 20 minutes to complete on the platform. It is designed to fit smoothly alongside school homework, extracurriculars, and exams without causing stress.
